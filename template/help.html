How to Use the Paraphrasing Tool

Step 1: Enter Text

Once you reach the website, you'll find a text box. Enter the text you want to paraphrase in this box.

Step 2: Click 'Paraphrase' Button

After entering your text, click on the 'Paraphrase Text' button below the text box to generate the paraphrased output.

Step 3: View Paraphrased Output

The paraphrased text will appear in the second box below the 'Paraphrase Text' button.

Step 4: Copy or Download Output

You can copy the paraphrased text by clicking the 'Copy' button or download it as a text (.txt) or PDF file.
